Task-Relevant Maturity Assessment

Answer these questions based on the employee's capability for this specific task.

1. Experience: How many times has this person completed similar tasks?

2. Execution Quality: Rate their current execution quality for this task

3. Autonomy: How much guidance do they currently need?

4. Problem-Solving: When issues arise with this task, what happens?

5. Understanding: Do they understand the "why" behind this task?

6. Adaptability: How do they handle variations or new contexts for this task?
